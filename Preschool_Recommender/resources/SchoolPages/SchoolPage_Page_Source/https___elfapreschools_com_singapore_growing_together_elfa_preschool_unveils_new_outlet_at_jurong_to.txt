</div><div class="fusion-text fusion-text-3"><p>Located in the heart of Jurong, ELFA Preschool’s new outlet is within walking distance to major transportation nodes. This includes multiple bus options, Jurong East and Clementi MRT, as well as a nearby multi-storey carpark for drivers’ convenient parking.</p>
<p>The strategic location of the preschool places it close to communal amenities such as Jurong Regional Library, Science Centre Singapore, and The Chevrons. ELFA Preschool @ Jurong is also in proximity to malls such as IMM, JEM, and Westgate. The preschool is located directly above an NTUC FairPrice, and nearby neighbourhood playgrounds.</p>
<p>This conveniences parents in utilising these amenities, while our children benefit from immersing in the local community during their daily walks and outdoor play. With carefully curated learning environments from infant care till Kindergarten Two, the new outlet offers the same high standards of facilities and amenities. Children can look forward to specially designed developmental areas for dramatic play, gross motor area, and creative art.</p>
