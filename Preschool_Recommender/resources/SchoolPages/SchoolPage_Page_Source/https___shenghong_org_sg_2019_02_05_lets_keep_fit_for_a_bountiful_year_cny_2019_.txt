                                <h1>“Let’s KEEP FIT for a bountiful year” – CNY 2019</h1>
                                <div class="group"></div>
                            </div>
                            <div class="document"> <p>HSHFSC ushered in the lunar new year early this year with a community event, aptly titled, “Let’s Keep fit for a Bountiful Year – CNY 2019”.</p>
<p>An interactive mass Zumba workout for the young and old kickstarted the event. Participants were treated to a string of enjoyable and exciting stage performances. They also took part in fun-filled activities at the game stalls and enjoyed healthy finger food at the eatery booths. Overall, Let’s Keep fit for a Bountiful Year – CNY 2019 had achieved the objective of bringing together the community to enjoy the festivities, with an emphasis on having a healthy living lifestyle.</p>
