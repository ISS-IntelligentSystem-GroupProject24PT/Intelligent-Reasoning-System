																																	<h3 class="">Job Description</h3>
<p>The Senior Nurse in a Senior Care Centre shall also undertake the secondary appointment as a Care Programme Associate.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<h3 class="">Key Responsibilities</h3>
<ul>
<li>Take charge of the entire clinical nursing practice in a Senior Care Centre</li>
<li>Take charge of nursing care for elderly clients; including fall risks and hospitalisation processes.</li>
<li>Take charge of medical procedures in an outbreak of infectious diseases.</li>
<li>Conduct client assessments during admissions and periodic reviews.</li>
<li>Participate in multidisciplinary case discussions.</li>
<li>Oversee adequacy of medical supplies and equipment.</li>
</ul>
<h3 class="">Job Holder Requirements and Qualifications</h3>
<ul>
<li>Diploma in Nursing – Registered with the Singapore Nursing Board</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of nursing experience in a hospital or eldercare setting</li>
<li>Good communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Possess a passionate disposition to care for frail seniors</li>
<li>Be able to conduct care programmes for seniors; e.g. daily news/healthcare reviews/cooking therapy/arts &amp; craft/singing/dancing, reminiscence therapy; etc.</li>
</ul>
