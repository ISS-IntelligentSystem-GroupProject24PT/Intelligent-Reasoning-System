                                <h1>Our Services</h1>

                                <div class="group"></div>

                            </div>

                            <div class="document"> <p><span style="color: #961ced; font-size: 14pt;"><b>Life Point provides the following services (<a href="#mandarin">中文</a>):&nbsp;</b></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>1</strong><strong>. Workshops</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Life Review Workshop:</strong> to facilitate the elderly to reflect and reminiscent their life journey so as to heal the past hurt and to receive affirmation on their life experience in a closed group setting.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Towards A Good Death Workshop:</strong> to facilitate the elderly to explore the meaning of life and death, do forward planning on end of life matters for peace of mind in a closed group setting.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>2. One-stop services&nbsp;</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Public talks: to conduct regular educational talks on topics related to end-of-life (EOL) matters such as Lasting Power of Attorney(LPA), Will, Advance Care Plan(ACP) and Advance Medical Directive(AMD). Subject matter expertise will be invited to share relevant information on the subject.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Provide assistance for seniors to complete EOL related documents at our centre.</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>3</strong><strong>. Outreaching</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">To work with community partners to identify the needs of the elderly in terms of awareness and preparation on End-of-Life matters.</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>4. Embracing Li</strong><strong>fe Series</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">At Life Point, we strongly advocate for Live Well, Leave Well. We encourage Living Well by organising various activities for seniors to participate in. Seniors who choose to join us as members will&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 14pt;">receive updates on activities and a quarterly e-newsletter to keep them informed on topics related to both “Living Well” and “Leaving Well”.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">These activities include, but are not limited to:</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Excursion &amp; visits: </strong>For building friendship and connection among seniors, as well as for educational purposes, such as visits to funeral parlors, heritage trails, Esplanade etc</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Interest groups:</strong> small groups connect elderly with common interests together and to engage the elderly in meaningful activities such as Reading Club and Rummikub.</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Senior Volunteerism: </strong>volunteering opportunities include organising member’s activities, help on administrative work and befriend with isolated/lonely seniors</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Besides the above, we also collaborate with National Silver Academy (NSA) to run courses that we believe will enrich a senior’s life.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">These courses include:</span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Autobiography Writing</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Japanese Pastel Art (Nagomi)</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Brain Workshop</strong></span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Silver Years</strong></span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong><span style="color: #961ced;">生命同行坊提供以下服务：</span></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong><br>
</strong><strong>1.学习小组</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>生命回顾学习小组：</strong>在彼此尊重的小组里，引导长者回顾人生，抚平旧伤，并为自己走过独一无二的人生路，接受一份诚挚的肯定。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>规划善终学习小组：</strong>在彼此尊重的小组里，引导长者学习生死课题和生命的意义，为善终提早做准备，以期达到心安无憾的人生。</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>2. 一站式服务</strong></span></p>
<ul>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>教育讲座：</strong>定期主办善终准备的公共教育讲座，例如持久授权书，遗嘱，预先护理计划以及预先医疗指示。 有关课题专家将为您提供可靠的相关资讯 。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;">我们在方便的地点，协助长者安排签署以上文件。</span></li>
</ul>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>3.&nbsp;</strong><strong>社区合作</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">与社区伙伴合作，共同探讨为年长者提供善终的教育及准备提供协助。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>4. 拥</strong><strong>抱人生系列 (成为会员）</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">长者受鼓励加入会员籍以加入会员活动。会员将收到每月简讯和季度会讯从而获知活动讯息。活动包括：</span></p>
<ol>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>传承智慧：</strong>支持长者传承生活智慧给予下一代，协助长者撰写自传并出版合集就是一项社会教育计划。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>郊游与参访</strong>：让长者通过学习性质的活动建立友谊，彼此连结，包括参观殡仪馆生死教育课，认识古迹文化之旅，以及滨海艺术文化中心等。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>兴趣小组：</strong>让志同道合的长者组成不同的兴趣小组，通过有意义的活动连结彼此，例如读书会。</span></li>
<li><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>长者义工： </strong>提供长者参与义务工作的机会，例如主持会员活动，协助行政工作以及关怀独居老人。</span></li>
</ol>
