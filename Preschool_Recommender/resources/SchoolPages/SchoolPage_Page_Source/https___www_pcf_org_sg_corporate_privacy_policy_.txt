							<h3>Introduction</h3>
						<p>At PAP Community Foundation (“PCF”), we respect the privacy and confidentiality of personal data in our possession or under our control. We have implemented policies and practices to safeguard the collection, use, disclosure, storage and other processing of personal data provided to us.</p>
											
										
							<h3>How We Collect Your Personal Data</h3>
						<p>We collect personal data when we perform one or several of the following services for you:</p>
<p><b>Sparkletots</b>:</p>
<ul>
<li>Registering your interest to enrol your child at one of our Centres</li>
<li>Submitting your application to ECDA for subsidy approval</li>
<li>Confirming your child’s enrolment at one of our Centres</li>
<li>When referred by ECDA to contact you for student enrolment at one of our Centres</li>
<li>Invoicing student fees monthly</li>
<li>Administrating any student refund fees due to you</li>
<li>Request us to manage any food and medication for your child</li>
<li>When you, or any person authorised by you, take your child home at our Centres</li>
<li>Participate in any commercial or external events or programs organised for your child</li>
</ul>
<p><b>Sparkle Care</b><b>:</b></p>
<ul>
<li>Register your interest to enrol for services offered by our centres, including initial assessments and admission</li>
<li>When we apply your application for eligible subsidies or financial assistance</li>
<li>When administering medication or medical care, including necessary actions such as hospital transfers and instructions from hospital/ clinic or next of kin</li>
<li>When making or receiving referrals to or from other service providers</li>
<li>When processing registration fees, payments, donations or other billing purposes</li>
<li>When participating in internal or external events and activities</li>
<li>Through client satisfaction questionnaires to improve our services</li>
<li>Through website interactions, as well as direct communication via various channels including walk-in enquiries</li>
<li>To contact caregiver or next of kin for emergency situations</li>
<li>Informing clients, caregivers and volunteers about new services, events or special activities</li>
<li>Managing photos and videos for publicity purposes</li>
<li>When engaging in community screening, outreach and research projects that involve interaction with clients, caregivers and volunteers</li>
</ul>
<p><b>General: </b></p>
<ul>
<li>Responding to our events or campaigns</li>
<li>Providing feedback to us on our quality of service or your user experience</li>
<li>Visit our websites and leave behind your contact information</li>
<li>Communicating with us via emails or written correspondence</li>
<li>Register at any of our reception counters</li>
<li>Submit your CV and job application form to us in response to our recruitment advertisements</li>
<li>Receive your CV from recruitment firms or job portals, which are forwarded to or retrieved by us</li>
</ul>
											
										
							<h3>Types of Personal Data that We Collect about you</h3>
						<p>The types of personal data we collect about you include:</p>
<ul>
<li>Child/Parents/Guardians personal details (Name, Address, NRIC No., Birth certificate, Contact No., Email Address, etc.</li>
<li>Student’s preferences (Likes, Dislikes, Favourite food, Medication or Service Preference)</li>
<li>Job Applicant’s personal details (Name, NRIC No. / FIN No. / Passport No., Gender, Date of Birth, Race, Citizenship, Nationality, Marital Status, Religion, Languages spoken or written, Household / Personal Income)</li>
<li>Job Applicant’s educational and professional qualifications (Highest Education Level, Qualifications, Schools Attended, Academic Transcripts, Membership of Professional Bodies)</li>
<li>Job applicant’s professional and work experience (Job title, Occupation, Employment history, Work experience, Curriculum Vitae)</li>
</ul>
											
										
							<h3>How We Use your Personal Data</h3>
						<p>We use the personal data you provide us for one or more of the following purposes:</p>
<ul>
<li>Processing registration/enrolment</li>
<li>Processing billing, payment refunds and other credit-related activities</li>
<li>Processing payment for services, purchases and transactions</li>
<li>Processing and administering student awards</li>
<li>Processing job applications, recruitment and selection</li>
<li>Conducting direct activities and joint programmes with other service providers</li>
<li>Enhancing experience and improving our services</li>
<li>Managing food and medication administration</li>
<li>Managing student/clients care and account</li>
<li>Communicating with students/parents/guardians/clients/ caregiver &amp; website visitors</li>
<li>Notifying parents/guardians/clients/caregiver about services, special events, and programmes</li>
<li>Responding to inquiries and feedback to improve our quality of service</li>
<li>Monitoring the movement of visitors to our physical premises</li>
<li>Carrying out our obligations arising from any contracts entered into between you and us</li>
<li>Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements</li>
</ul>
											
										
							<h3>Who We Disclose Personal Data To</h3>
						<p>We disclose some of the personal data you provide us to the following parties or organisations outside PCF in order to fulfil our services to you:</p>
<ul>
<li>Regulatory Authorities e.g. ECDA (Early Childhood Development Agency)</li>
<li>IT/Technical Support, IT Service Providers</li>
<li>Banks, Payment card processing companies</li>
<li>Government agencies</li>
</ul>
											
										
							<h3>How We Manage the Collection, Use, Disclosure and Storage of Your Personal Data</h3>
																	
										
							<h3>Obtaining Consent</h3>

We will respond to your request as soon as reasonably possible and, in any event, we will respond to you within 30 days after receiving your request. We may charge a fee for processing your request, but we will let you know the amount of the fee before you incur it.</p>
<p>You may also ask us to correct an error or omission in the personal data we hold about you. Unless we are satisfied on reasonable grounds that a correction should not be made, we will correct the personal data as soon as practicable.</p>
<p>When you make any such request, we may need to verify your identity – for example, by checking your identity card number or other legal identification document.</p>

<p>From time to time, we may do a verification exercise for you to update us on any changes to the personal data we hold about you. If we are in an ongoing relationship with you, it is important that you update us if there are any changes in the personal data we hold about you (such as a change in your home address).</p>

<p>Entities that provide services to us to process personal data on our behalf will be bound by contracts. They must also take reasonable steps to ensure compliance with those measures.</p>
