<h1 style="margin: 0px 0px 0.8em; padding: 0px; font-family: 'Times New Roman', Times, serif; font-weight: normal; color: #e20a16; font-size: 2.5em; text-align: center;"><strong>Embark on a Career with Hampton Pre-School&nbsp;Tanjong Pagar</strong></h1>
<p style="margin: 0px 0px 1.5em; padding: 0px; line-height: 1.5; text-align: center; color: #666666; font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif; font-size: 12px;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">If you are dynamic and passionate individual with a positive mindset,&nbsp;</span><span style="font-size: 12pt;">we welcome you to join our team.</span></p>
<h2><strong>Infant Care Teacher</strong></h2>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Job Role &amp; Responsibilities</strong></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Undertake the teaching of a class in accordance with the school’s agreed policies and guidelines.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Plan and carry out the daily programme, developmental and caregiving tasks as assigned.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Create meaningful language opportunities for children where love for Mandarin can be promoted.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Document children’s learning through contributions towards newsletters, class panels and children’s portfolios.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Attend to the health, hygiene and safety needs of the infants.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Create a safe environment and provide the best possible care to infants under their care.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Build and maintain good rapport with parents.</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Obtained at least a Certificate in Infant/Toddler Care and Development, or Fundamentals Certificate in Early Childhood Care and Education (with a certificate in Infant/Toddler Care) or its equivalent</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● First Aid Certificate recognized by ECDA</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Minimum 2 years experience in a similar setting</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">● Good interpersonal and communication skills</p>
<p style="margin: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>Class Teacher</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 0px;"><strong style="font-size: 12.16px;">Job Description</strong></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Responsible to:&nbsp;</strong>Principal in the execution of professional duties and responsibilities.</p>
<ol style="list-style-type: upper-alpha;">
<li><strong>English/Class Teacher to</strong>:</li>
<li><strong>Team Member to</strong>:</li>
</ol>
<ul>
<li>Respect and value each child’s social, cultural and religious background and by example, provide mutual respect and tolerance.</li>
<li>Share in the management of pastoral care, health, welfare and behaviour of all pupils and in particular, those within your designated class group.</li>
<li>Undertake the teaching of a class in accordance with the school’s agreed policies and guidelines.</li>
<li>Use a variety of appropriate teaching and caring methods to ensure high quality learning takes place.</li>
<li>Provide planning documents identifying specific learning objectives, differentiated tasks, special needs, extension and assessment, to the Principal or other authorized officer.</li>
<li>Maintain professional dialogue with colleagues concerning the children, other colleagues and the school. Treat all information with confidentiality.</li>
<li>Ensure the provision of equal opportunities for all.</li>
<li>Encourage children to take pride in their own environment and show respect and concern for others, to the benefit of the school as a caring community.</li>
<li>Be responsible for all aspects of classroom management eg. Care of equipment with resources well-labelled and accessible. Maintain a tidy, well organised room.</li>
<li>Create an interesting and lively classroom which promotes independent learning.</li>
<li>Collaborate with Principal and staff in establishing and developing effective pupil assessment procedures and moderation portfolios. Keep appropriate records of achievement for each child in class and provide reports as necessary.</li>
<li>Build positive links between yourself and parents of children in your care.</li>
<li>Guide, motivate and monitor classroom helpers in carrying out their tasks.</li>
</ul>
<ul>
<li>Collaborate with staff in long-term planning.</li>
<li>Take corporate responsibility for displays throughout the school.</li>
<li>Share management and delivery of assemblies.</li>
<li>Offer and receive advice and guidance on issues that affect the whole school’s performance and thos.</li>
<li>Share your expertise.</li>
<li>Participate in appraisal procedures and in-service training that provide personal, professional, and career development.</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>At least 1-2 years of relevant professional teaching experience in early childhood;</li>
<li>The candidate should hold a minimum of a Diploma in Early Childhood or Education or equivalent</li>
<li>Knowledge of or willingness to acquire knowledge of inquiry-based learning is required;</li>
<li>Applicants must provide original copies of their Certificates and academic transcripts for authentication;</li>
<li>L2 Certificate (English)&nbsp;</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>Assistant Teacher</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Job Description</strong></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">The Assistant Teacher will assist the English/Mandarin&nbsp;Teacher and the Principal in the execution of professional duties and responsibilities.</p>
<ul>
<li>Working alongside English/Mandarin Teacher, provide appropriate learning experiences for&nbsp;children within an inquiry, play-based context</li>
<li>Assist the English/Mandarin Teacher in documenting the children’s learning through&nbsp;contributions via newsletters, portfolios etc.</li>
<li>Create a warm and nurturing environment for children</li>
<li>Build and maintain close partnership with parent</li>
<li>协助英文/中文老师建立分组式活动、探索为主导、娱乐为背景的教学模式；</li>
<li>具有积极的团队合作力；</li>
<li>保持与家长的良好沟通；</li>
<li>协助英文/中文老师通过校园通讯、班级告示板及儿童成长观察报告记录孩子的学习经历；</li>
<li>提供积极的语言环境，激发孩子学习华文的热情。</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Possess a Diploma in Early Childhood Care and Education or its&nbsp;equivalent with at least 1 year of relevant&nbsp;teaching experience are welcome to apply.</li>
<li>Good communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Able to work well independently as well as within a team</li>
<li>幼儿教育文凭，持有本科相关学历者优先；</li>
<li>ECDA L2</li>
<li>至少1年幼儿教学经验；</li>
<li>普通话标准，写作规范；</li>
<li>工作积极，有责任感。</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
<h2><strong>Mandarin Teacher</strong></h2>
<p style="margin: 0px;">&nbsp;</p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Job Description</strong></p>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">The Mandarin Language Teacher will be responsible for taking care of a class of young children and facilitating small group experiences within an inquiry, play-based context in the school.</p>
<ul>
<li>Plan engaging circle time experiences and small group experiences within an inquiry, play-based context</li>
<li>Document children’s learning through contributions towards newsletters, class panels and children’s portfolios</li>
<li>Create meaningful language opportunities for children where love for Mandarin can be promoted;</li>
<li>Build and maintain a close partnership with parents</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin-bottom: 16px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;">华文教师主要负责照看孩子，引导孩子以探索为主导，寓教于乐。</p>
<ul>
<li>创造温暖、关怀及培育性的环境；</li>
<li>建立分组式活动、探索为主导、娱乐为背景的教学模式；</li>
<li>具有积极的团队合作力；</li>
<li>保持与家长的良好沟通；</li>
<li>通过校园通讯、班级告示板及儿童成长观察报告记录孩子的学习经历；</li>
<li>提供积极的语言环境，激发孩子学习华文的热情。</li>
</ul>
<p style="margin-bottom: 0px; margin-left: 0px; margin-top: 0px;"><strong>Qualifications</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Diploma in Early Childhood Care &amp; Education in Teaching (Chinese language) or its equivalent</li>
<li>At least 2 years of relevant working experience in a preschool or childcare setting is required</li>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Able to work well independently as well as within a team</li>
<li>Required to speak, read, and write in Mandarin as the candidates need to teach the students Mandarin Language.</li>
<li>ECDA L2 certification</li>
<li>幼儿教育大专文凭，持有本科相关学历者优先；</li>
<li>至少2年幼儿教学经验；</li>
<li>普通话标准，写作规范；</li>
<li>工作积极，有责任感。</li>
<li>ECDA L2 certification</li>
</ul>
