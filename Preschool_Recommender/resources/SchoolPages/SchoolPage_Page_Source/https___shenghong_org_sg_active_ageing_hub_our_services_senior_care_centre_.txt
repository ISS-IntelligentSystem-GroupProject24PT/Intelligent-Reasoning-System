                                <h1>Day Care Centre</h1>

                                <div class="group"></div>

                            </div>

                            <div class="document"> <p><span style="font-size: 10pt; color: #800000;"><strong>(中文如下)</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>The only Senior Care Centre in Singapore that offers Day Care from as low as $32 a month inclusive of transportation and Rehabilitation for $1 per session for low income families.&nbsp;</strong></span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 14pt;"><strong><u>Day Care&nbsp;</u></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 14pt;">Provides our seniors with opportunities to socialize&nbsp;with members of different age groups.&nbsp; Through opportunities in communication, companionship and opportunities to take charge of activities of living, our seniors’ general and social well-being can be optimized. The service also includes activities and exercises with the objective to keep seniors engaged and physically active where possible. The centre also provides morning beverages, lunch and afternoon-tea time snacks.</span></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16821" src="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2.jpg" alt="" width="771" height="240" srcset="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2.jpg 771w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2-300x93.jpg 300w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2-768x239.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 771px) 100vw, 771px"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 14pt; color: #ff0000; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;"><strong>$32 ONLY</strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt; color: #000000; font-family: verdana, geneva, sans-serif;"><strong> for Monthly Day Care fee</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">Most of our low income members pay ONLY $32 for day care with lunch and two ways transportation. This greatly help their working families to save money on daily lunch which cost about $3.50-6 per meal, equilavent to $77-$132 a month excluding transportation fees and other activity related fees.&nbsp;</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 14pt;"><strong><u>Transport Service</u></strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif; font-size: 14pt;">Transport will be provided based on availability of resources and route.&nbsp; Terms and conditions apply for members who are interested in transportation.</span></p>
<p><img class="wp-image-16787 alignleft" src="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/1.jpeg" alt="" width="363" height="272" srcset="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/1.jpeg 1600w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/1-300x225.jpeg 300w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/1-768x576.jpeg 768w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/1-1024x768.jpeg 1024w" sizes="(max-width: 363px) 100vw, 363px"></p>
<p><img class="" src="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/HWA-1-van.jpeg" width="372" height="279"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 24pt;"><strong>我们的服务</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">新加坡唯一中心为低收入家庭提供每月 32 元的日托服务和每节 1 元的康复治疗服务。</span></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline; font-size: 18pt;"><strong>日托</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">为我们的受益人提供与不同年龄段的成员社交的机会。 通过交流、陪伴和负责生活活<span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif;">动</span></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><span style="font-family: georgia, palatino, serif;">的</span>机会，我们的长者可以得到优化。 该服务还包括活动，目的是让长者在可能的情况下保持身体活跃。 中心还提供早间饮料、午餐和下午茶点心。</span></p>
<p><img class="aligncenter size-full wp-image-16821" src="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2.jpg" alt="" width="771" height="240" srcset="https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2.jpg 771w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2-300x93.jpg 300w, https://shenghong.org.sg/wp-content/uploads/2020/10/Daycare-2-768x239.jpg 768w" sizes="(max-width: 771px) 100vw, 771px"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">日托费每个月只需</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">&nbsp;</span></strong></span><span style="font-size: 18pt;"><strong><span style="color: #ff0000;">$32</span></strong></span></p>
