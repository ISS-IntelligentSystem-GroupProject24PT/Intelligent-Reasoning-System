<p><strong>Our Professional Educators</strong> <br>Our teachers play the important roles of facilitator and motivator who:</p>
<ul>
<li>Apply <strong>age and developmentally appropriate</strong> practices innovatively to develop the diverse strengths of each individual child.</li>
<li>Engage children in meaningful learning experiences in collaboration with their peers and adults (parents/community collaborations).</li>
<li>Provide a welcoming, warm, loving, <strong>experiential, stimulating, secure</strong> and safe environment so that your child would be encouraged to explore, inquire, interact, develop positive dispositions and independence.</li>
<li>Are a good role models (morals and ethics) for children.</li>
</ul>
