<h3><span style="font-size: 18pt;"><strong>CORPORATE INFORMATION</strong></span></h3>
<div>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">Hampton Pre-School is a PAP Community Foundation (PCF) child-care centre managed by EtonHouse. Hampton Pre-School commenced operations in December 2009, under the initiative of our then Deputy Prime Minister Mr. Wong Kan Seng and the Bishan East PAP Community Foundation to bring quality pre-school educational services to the community. This is the first PCF child-care centre managed by a private education provider. Hampton Pre-School receives direct support in terms of its programme delivery, classroom set-up and pedagogical support from EtonHouse. Mrs. Ng Gim Choo, founder and Managing Director of EtonHouse International Education Group said at the opening of Hampton Pre-School, “EtonHouse has always believed in providing the best educational experience to children. We are happy to be able to contribute to the community by sharing our expertise in the early childhood domain. We hope to offer a high quality pre-school experience at Hampton that gives children a strong early foundation across all domains of development”</span></p>
<h3><span style="font-size: 18pt;">VISION &amp; MISSION</span></h3>
<h3><span style="font-size: 18pt;"><span style="color: #000000;">Vision</span>&nbsp;</span></h3>

<h3><span style="font-size: 18pt;"><span style="color: #000000;">Mission</span>&nbsp;&nbsp;</span></h3>

<p><span style="color: #000000; font-size: 12pt;">An educational philosophy that recognises young children as capable and competent learners</span></p>
</li>
<li>
