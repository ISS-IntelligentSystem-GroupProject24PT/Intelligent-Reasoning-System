		<div class="fl-rich-text">
	<p>Like in-centre classes, the purpose of our remote learning classes is to observe students while they work and only give guidance and instruction when needed. <p>Therefore, the values of independent learning, self-discipline, time management and self-confidence in solving problems that Kumon strives to instil are brought into focus and displayed while doing remote learning, both for school and Kumon.</p>
