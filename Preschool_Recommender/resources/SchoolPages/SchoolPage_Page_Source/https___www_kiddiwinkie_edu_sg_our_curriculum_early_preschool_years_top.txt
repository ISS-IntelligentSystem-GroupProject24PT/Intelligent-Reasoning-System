      <div class="paragraph-title-rich-text__title-rich-text-text"><p>The following specialised programmes are designed as an extension of our core programmes, to extend your child’s learning in a variety of key areas.</p>

<h2>Author’s Universe 大作家，小读者 (Nursery to Kindergarten 2)</h2>

<p>Designed to promote exposure to quality literature, Author’s Universe introduces children to authors and their storybooks of various genres, while developing key language acquisition skills in both English and Chinese languages.</p>

<p>Through exploration of story elements and a discovery of their own story preferences, children will try their hands at creating their own stories which will pave way towards their very own book publication at the end of the year.</p>

<h2>Pic-a-Book 图乐窝 (Playgroup &amp; Pre-Nursery)&nbsp;</h2>

<p>Curated specially for your young children’s literacy needs, Pic-a-Book incorporates imaginative play and storytelling from children’s point-of-view. By bringing classic story scenes alive, your young ones will get to explore verbal language through dramatisation in both English and Chinese, which expands their vocabulary and comprehension skills.</p>

<h2>Backpack Stories 背包旅游记 (Playgroup to Kindergarten 2)&nbsp;</h2>

<p>A multi-cultural immersion programme with elements of creative writing, Backpack Stories will take your children on fun “travels” around the world and expose them to various countries’ culture.</p>

<p>Here, your children will learn to appreciate and embrace other traditions and celebrate each country’s uniqueness. They will be encouraged to share their thoughts and experiences through age-appropriate journaling which is a building block to creative writing awareness.</p>

<h2>文化之窗 – Chinese Cultural Immersion Programme (Playgroup to Kindergarten 2 –&nbsp;<br>
Commencing Term 2, 2022)</h2>

<p>Conducted entirely in Chinese, this programme aims to encourage children across all levels to develop an interest in learning Chinese through exploration of the Chinese culture through art, food, festivals and more.&nbsp;</p>

<h2>Kiddi Newsroom 趣谈世界 (Nursery to Kindergarten 2)&nbsp;</h2>

<p>Kiddi Newsroom is our current affairs programme supported by Ninonews, a news and video content resource for young children. Through this programme, we aim to encourage children to build an awareness of affairs and issues happening in the community and around the world, while promoting the sharing and discussions on issues that are current, relevant and of interest to them.</p>

<p>Families will also be encouraged to play a part by lending a helping hand in the children’s preparation of discussions and sharing in class.</p>

<h2>小小“悦”读家 – Chinese Reading Programme (Nursery to Kindergarten 2)&nbsp;</h2>

<p>Our in-house Chinese Reading programme builds children’s Chinese language acquisition by promoting listening and reading skills.</p>

<p>Activities are conducted using carefully selected Chinese readers and supported with the use of Pen Pal Whizz, an electronic Smart Reading Pen. Supplemented by reading flashcards and questions for discussions, children will have plenty of opportunities to ensure reading readiness.</p>

<h2>Super Readers, Super Heroes (Nursery to Kindergarten 2)</h2>

<p>Designed to build English language acquisition skills, this programme provides a solid literacy foundation by building children’s reading confidence as they progress through various reading stages. Above that, children will also explore writing through journaling, which allows them to build on their reading comprehension skills.</p>

      <div class="paragraph-title-rich-text__title-rich-text-text"><p>To encourage holistic development, the following selection of enrichment programmes are offered to our children.</p>

<h2>FIITercise</h2>

<p>FIITercise is our gym and sports programme which introduces children to fundamental movement skills and character building.&nbsp;</p>

<p>We cover 6 different types of sports, ranging from ball, sticks and racket games. During the sessions, our trainers grab every opportunity to inculcate positive values such as sportsmanship, teamwork and resilience in our young ones.</p>

<h2>Moving Melodies</h2>

<p>Moving Melodies by Dance Theatre Arts is a uniquely crafted music and movement programme for children aged 1.5 to 6 years. The activities are curated to develop the vestibular and proprioceptive systems in preschoolers.&nbsp;</p>

<p>A well-functioning system in these areas lays the foundation for young children to transition into more complex activities such as reading and writing.</p>

<h2>Artsy Fingers</h2>

<p>Artsy Fingers focuses on age-appropriate themes while touching on the elements of art.</p>

<p>For starters, your child will explore art through the songs and rhymes that he/she is familiar with. As the term progresses, we introduce the works of famous artists and children will get to create artworks based on various styles and concepts.</p>

<h2>Third Language Enrichment</h2>

<p>At selected campuses, we offer Japanese, Korean and French lessons. Conducted by native teachers, these lessons are extended to both local and international children.</p>

<h2>Tech Crafters</h2>

<p>Available exclusively at our Jurong Gateway, East Coast and Mountbatten centres, Tech Crafters is an embedded enrichment programme offered as part of the innovative curriculum. Through unplugged coding activities, children are given opportunities to develop problem-solving skills using logical and structural thinking.</p>

<p>With Tech Crafters, children will tap into their prior knowledge and reasoning skills to make sense of the problem and exercise their creativity in solving them.<br>
