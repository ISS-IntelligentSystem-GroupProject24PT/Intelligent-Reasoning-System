<p>Little Skool-House is more than just a “school”. The invented “Skool” in our name reflects our belief that focusing solely on correctness, mechanics, and memorization is not conducive to teaching preschoolers to write. As children learn to write, they often use their understanding of letters, sounds, and spelling patterns to create spellings for words. Studies show that this process of invented spelling helps children comprehend the relationships between speech sounds and the alphabet. </p>



<p>Our bilingual curriculum celebrates discovery, creativity, and diversity, nurturing your child’s love for exploring literature, math, science, and the world around them. We also focus on routines, healthy habits, empathy, and global awareness to cultivate adaptable, creative problem solvers, and global citizens.</p>
