<h1 style="text-align:center;">FAQs</h1><div id="Content_C047_Col00" class="sf_colsIn container" data-sf-element="Container" data-placeholder-label="Container">
<div class="accordion" id="accordionExample">
    <div id="Content_C042_Col00" class="sf_colsIn sub-section " data-sf-element="Accordion Content" data-placeholder-label="Accordion Content">

    <div class="accordion-item" id="why_must_i_visit_a_certificate_issuer_ci_to_certify_my_lpa">
        <h2 class="accordion-header" id="heading_why_must_i_visit_a_certificate_issuer_ci_to_certify_my_lpa">
            <button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapse_why_must_i_visit_a_certificate_issuer_ci_to_certify_my_lpa" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse_why_must_i_visit_a_certificate_issuer_ci_to_certify_my_lpa" aria-label="Why must I visit a Certificate Issuer (CI) to certify my LPA?">
                Why must I visit a Certificate Issuer (CI) to certify my LPA?
            </button>
        </h2>
        <div id="collapse_why_must_i_visit_a_certificate_issuer_ci_to_certify_my_lpa" class="accordion-collapse collapse" aria-labelledby="heading_why_must_i_visit_a_certificate_issuer_ci_to_certify_my_lpa" data-bs-parent="#accordionExample">
            <div class="accordion-body">
                <p>The CI signs on the LPA Form as a witness for the Donor, to certify that the Donor understands the purpose of the LPA and the scope of authority granted to Donees.</p>
<p>The CI also ensures that there is no fraud or undue pressure used to induce the Donor to make the LPA.</p>
<p>More information on CIs can be found <a href="http://www.msf.gov.sg/what-we-do/opg/lasting-power-of-attorney/where-to-find-a-certificate-issuer"><span class="visually-hidden">Click here to read more</span>here</a>.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>



    <div class="accordion-item" id="must_the_donee_be_present_when_the_lpa_certificate_issuer_witnesses_and_certifies_the_lpa">
        <h2 class="accordion-header" id="heading_must_the_donee_be_present_when_the_lpa_certificate_issuer_witnesses_and_certifies_the_lpa">
            <button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapse_must_the_donee_be_present_when_the_lpa_certificate_issuer_witnesses_and_certifies_the_lpa" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse_must_the_donee_be_present_when_the_lpa_certificate_issuer_witnesses_and_certifies_the_lpa" aria-label="Must the Donee be present when the LPA Certificate Issuer witnesses and certifies the LPA?">
                Must the Donee be present when the LPA Certificate Issuer witnesses and certifies the LPA?
            </button>
        </h2>
        <div id="collapse_must_the_donee_be_present_when_the_lpa_certificate_issuer_witnesses_and_certifies_the_lpa" class="accordion-collapse collapse" aria-labelledby="heading_must_the_donee_be_present_when_the_lpa_certificate_issuer_witnesses_and_certifies_the_lpa" data-bs-parent="#accordionExample">
            <div class="accordion-body">
                There is no requirement for the Donee to be present when the certificate issuer witnesses and certifies on the LPA. However, some certificate issuers may prefer that the Donees are present. Hence, it would be best to check with the certificate issuer beforehand.
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>



    <div class="accordion-item" id="if_i_have_already_made_a_will_do_i_still_need_an_lpa">
        <h2 class="accordion-header" id="heading_if_i_have_already_made_a_will_do_i_still_need_an_lpa">
            <button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapse_if_i_have_already_made_a_will_do_i_still_need_an_lpa" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse_if_i_have_already_made_a_will_do_i_still_need_an_lpa" aria-label="If I have already made a Will, do I still need an LPA?">
                If I have already made a Will, do I still need an LPA?
            </button>
        </h2>
        <div id="collapse_if_i_have_already_made_a_will_do_i_still_need_an_lpa" class="accordion-collapse collapse" aria-labelledby="heading_if_i_have_already_made_a_will_do_i_still_need_an_lpa" data-bs-parent="#accordionExample">
            <div class="accordion-body">
                <p>The LPA and Will are two separate legal documents. An LPA allows a person (Donor) to appoint one or more Donees to make decisions and act on his behalf if he should lose mental capacity one day. The LPA will be cancelled upon the death of the Donor.</p><p>A Will is to provide for the administration and distribution of what he owns (his estate) among his beneficiaries after his death.</p>
            </div>
        </div>
