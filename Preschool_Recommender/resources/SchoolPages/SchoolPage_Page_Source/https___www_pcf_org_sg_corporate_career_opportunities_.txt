																	<h3 class="">Key Responsibilities</h3>
<p>PCF is the leading Preschool Anchor Operator (AOP) in Singapore. In this regard, PCF aspires to be a responsible operator which upholds the highest standards of corporate governance as well as meets the stringent regulatory requirements and quality benchmarks as mandated by PCF’s Management, the Early Childhood Development Agency and other stakeholders. The incumbent is responsible for providing comprehensive support to the Preschool Team including Centre Principals (CPs), Executive Principals (EPs) and District Heads (DHs); ensuring operational excellence across our organization. This role involves collaborating with various stakeholders, contributing to the development and enhancement of operational procedures.</p>
<p><strong>1. Review SOPs, Policies, and Parents’ Handbook:</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Conduct regular reviews of Standard Operation Procedures (SOPs) and Parents’ Handbooks to ensure alignment with organizational goals.</li>
<li>Collaborate with stakeholders to initiate policy review and development processes.</li>
<li>Implement strategies to communicate the SOPs and Handbooks to the targeted audience and foster alignment in practices across all centres.</li>
<li>Develop new SOPs that guide Centres on operational and service standards.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>2. Staff Support and Guidance</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Act as a point of contact for staff enquiries, concerns, and operational challenges.</li>
<li>Provide guidance and assistance to address operational-related issues.</li>
<li>Foster a positive and collaborative work environment with the Preschool Team.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>3. Support New and Newly Promoted Centre Principals (CPs):</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Review induction guidelines.</li>
<li>Assist in the onboarding process for new CPs, including the newly promoted ones, to ensure a smooth transition.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>4. Implement Project</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Work with a team on projects such as Infant Project.</li>
<li>Develop project timelines, milestones and deliverables for project rollout.</li>
<li>Track project progress to ensure adherence to deadlines.</li>
<li>Coordinate meetings, training and workshops and events related to the project.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>5. Collaboration with Partners</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Establish and maintain collaborative relationships with external partners.</li>
<li>Coordinate and facilitate joint initiatives to enhance operational efficiency.</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>6. Admin/Procurement</strong></p>
<ul>
<li>Oversee administrative tasks and support procurement processes.</li>
<li>Maintain records, track expenditures, and ensure compliance with procurement policies.</li>
</ul>

<p>Qualifications: Diploma/Degree in Early Childhood/Child Psychology/Education or background in regulation or quality assurance.</p>
<p>Experience: At least 2 years of relevant working experience. Those with experience in Early Childhood will have an added advantage.</p>
<p>Other Qualities, Knowledge &amp; Skills:</p>
<ul>
<li>Excellent communication and interpersonal skills.</li>
<li>Excellent written skills with the ability to pay attention to details and feedback.</li>
<li>Ability to conduct research and gather relevant information. Knowledge of Early Childhood will be an added advantage.</li>
<li>Resilient and able to work in varied settings and fast-paced environment.</li>
<li>High ethical standards with the ability to maintain confidentiality.</li>
<li>Candidates with relevant experience may be considered for a more senior role.</li>

																																<div class="s_copy">
																	<p>PAP Community Foundation has relief schemes that allow you to join us on a short-term basis to support senior care centres in need of manpower.</p>
<p>We welcome you to join the PCF Relief Pool as:</p>
<h3>Relief Care Support Assistant</h3>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Job Description:</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Care for the health, well-being and safety of elderly clients [at all times] in the Senior Care Centre</li>
<li>Deliver personal care needs [including toileting support] to elderly clients, including clients with dementia</li>
<li>Support the conduct of programme activities for elderly clients</li>
<li>Participate in keeping the environment clean and equipment in good order</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Minimum Requirements:</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced/Higher/Certificate in Community &amp; Social Service [Senior Care Services] and good “O” level [minimum 3 credits] or WSQ Higher Certificate in Healthcare Support [Nursing care]</li>
</ul>
<h3>Relief Care Programme Coordinator</h3>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Job Description:</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Care for the well-being and safety of elderly clients [at all times] in the Senior Care Centre</li>
<li>Prepare and conduct programme activities for elderly clients</li>
<li>Deliver care needs to Persons-with-Dementia with Person-Centered Care [PCC] efforts and considerations</li>
<li>Organise social and recreational group excursions</li>
<li>Update Individual Care Plans and relevant records daily</li>
<li>Support caregivers and volunteers in caring for clients</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Minimum Requirements:</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Advanced/Higher/Certificate in Community &amp; Social Service [Senior Care Services] and good “O” level [minimum 3 credits] or WSQ Higher Certificate in Healthcare Support [Nursing care]</li>
<li>Competency in eldercare service with at least 2 years of working experience in an eldercare institution</li>
</ul>
<h3>Relief Senior Nurse(Locum)</h3>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Job Description:</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Take charge of the entire clinical nursing practice in a Senior Care Centre</li>
<li>Take charge of nursing care for elderly clients; including fall risks and hospitalization processes</li>
<li>Take charge of medical procedures in an outbreak of infectious diseases</li>
<li>Conduct client assessments during admissions and periodic reviews</li>
<li>Participate in multidisciplinary case discussions</li>
<li>Oversee adequacy of medical supplies and equipment</li>
</ul>
<p><span style="text-decoration: underline;">Minimum Requirements:</span></p>
<ul>
<li>Diploma in Nursing – Registered with the Singapore Nursing Board</li>
<li>Minimum 5 years of nursing experience in a hospital or eldercare setting</li>
<li>Good communication and interpersonal skills</li>
<li>Possess a passionate disposition to care for frail seniors</li>
<li>Be able to conduct care programmes for seniors; e.g. daily news/healthcare reviews/cooking therapy/arts &amp; crafts/singing/dancing, reminiscence therapy; etc.</li>
</ul>
<p>Please send your resume and required supporting document to <a href="mailto:Xin.Yi.Ng@pcf.org.sg" target="_blank" rel="noopener">Xin.Yi.Ng@pcf.org.sg</a>, indicating the position you are applying for.</p>
