	<p><span style="color: #963d97;"><strong>Cherie Hearts Nurtureloft</strong></span><b>&nbsp;</b>is a proud member of an awarding-winning home grown brand of child care centres that aims to provide premium childcare services.</p>
<p>At Cherie Hearts Nurtureloft Pte Ltd, we dedicate ourselves to providing quality child-focussed services for the children under our care in strong partnership with parents and the community. The Centre boasts of a large indoor playground and is well-equipped with facilities that provide children with a conducive environment to learn and grow. Our classrooms are rich in environment prints, play materials and different learning centres to facilitate children’s learning.</p>
<p>We have a strong emphasis on bilingual teaching in English and Mandarin to help our children build a strong foundation in their languages before they move on to primary education. Competencies in these skills are taught and learnt through a wide range of creative hands-on play activities.</p>
<p>Our teachers are friendly, committed, well-trained and experienced in the field of early childhood. With Early Childhood Education taking on a greater emphasis for its critical role in the formative and foundational years of a child, we ensure that our teachers remain sensitive to the changes in the field and constantly keep themselves abreast with professional upgrading.</p>
<p>Cherie Hearts Nurtureloft Pte Ltd provides a wide range of programmes and services to cater to the needs of our children and parents. The Centre is filled with meaningful and engaging activities all year round to boost the development of our children. Since its injunction in 2011, Cherie Hearts Nurtureloft Pte Ltd has won many Nation-wide prestigious awards, including the first prize in the 2014 National Day Pre-school Recycling Competition.</p>
