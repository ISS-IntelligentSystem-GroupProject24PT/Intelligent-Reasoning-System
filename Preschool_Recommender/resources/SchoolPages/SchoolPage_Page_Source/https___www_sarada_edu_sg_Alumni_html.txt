                          <td width="100%" height="613" colspan="2" valign="top"><h1 style="color:#000080">TAPAS: Teachers and parents   Association of Sarada</h1>
                            <p style="color:#000080"><br>
                                <strong>Dear   Teachers/ex-teachers/parents/ex-parents/ex-students of Sarada   Kindergarten.</strong><br>
                              &nbsp;
                              <br>
                              We would like to invite you to join the Teachers   and Parents Association of Sarada Kindergarten. (TAPAS) . This association is   the brainchild of a few present ad past parents and teachers of the Sarada   Kindergarten.<br>
                              &nbsp;
                              <br>
                              The main objects of the Association are detailed in the   constitution that has been submitted to the Registrar of Society for approval.&nbsp;   These are:<br>
                              &nbsp;                </p>
                            <ol>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To promote the advancement and improvement of   its members physically, intellectually, socially and morally.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To develop a true spirit of loyalty and love   for the Alma Mater.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To secure the co-operation and active interest   of pupils in carrying out aims and objectives of the Kindergarten.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To foster the ideals of the true citizenship   and to promote the feeling of comradeship among the races.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To promote the welfare of the Kindergarten, its   Students and Teachers.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To provide scholarship, prizes and financial   assistance to Students and Teachers of the Kindergarten.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To facilitate an active interaction between   parents and students in the various activities of the Kindergarten.</li>
                              <li style="color:#000080">To undertake various fund raising and other   activities for the support of the Kindergarten and/or the   Association.</li>
                            </ol>
                            <p style="color:#000080">We expect these objects will be translated into   activities such as workshops, seminars, sporting events, touring and other   social functions.&nbsp; In addition, we will expect to organize various fund raising   activities for the Kindergarten and also support the Kindergarten in its social   and other educational activities.</p>
                            <p style="color:#000080"><br>
                              The membership is open only to all present   and past, parents, teachers, principals, supervisors and ex-students over the   age of 18. All of the above individuals can only become members provided they   have been with the Sarada Kindergarten for a minimum period of 6   months.<br>
                              &nbsp;
                              <br>
                              Primarily there are three classes of membership, Ordinary (with   voting rights), Life (Ordinary members can convert to Life members on payment of   a fee) or Associate members (no voting rights and is open to students, teachers   and parents who cannot join as ordinary members).&nbsp;&nbsp; <br>
                              &nbsp;
                              <br>
                              The membership fee   for ordinary members is $12.00 per annum , or $30.00 for 3 years and for life   members, it is a one-time payment of $100.00.&nbsp; The membership fee for associate   members is $10.00 per annum.&nbsp;&nbsp;<br>
                              Please complete the <a href="TAPAS%20Application%20form.pdf">application form</a> and hand this over to the TAPAS Representatives.&nbsp;</p>
                            <p style="color:#000080">&nbsp;<br>
                              Thank you for your support,<br>
                              &nbsp;
                            <br>
                            Yours   sincerely,</p>
                          <p style="color:#000080">President </p>
                          <p style="color:#000080">(TAPAS)</p></td>
