                <div class="title text-center">
                    The Best Indoor Playgrounds in Singapore                </div>
                                    <div class="desc">
						<p>When the weather is unfavourable for outdoor play, it is time to turn to indoor playgrounds. Indoor playgrounds offer all the benefits of outdoor play - under shelter. So there’s no need to worry about overcast skies or thunderstorms; it’s time to play all day!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>We have curated a list of the best indoor playgrounds in Singapore for you and your child to explore. Go forth and have fun!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol>
<li>
<h3><a href="https://kiztopia.com/"><strong>Kiztopia&nbsp;</strong></a></h3>
</li>
</ol>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-349 size-full" src="https://sunbird.com.sg/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/KPG-03.jpg" alt="" width="600" height="500"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Kiztopia is one of the most popular indoor playgrounds in Singapore - and for good reason. There are three branches, but the largest is the Marine Square flagship outlet. This sprawling 18,000 square feet playground boasts 18 different playzones, offering various activities such as ball pits, train rides, trampolines, slides, and much more!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Location: Marina Square, 6 Raffles Boulevard #01-09, Singapore 039594</p>
<p>Opening Hours: Sun-Thur: 10am to 8pm; Fri &amp; Sat: 10am to 10pm</p>
<p>Admission fee: SGD$29.80 for one hour of play for an accompanying adult and one child</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol start="2">
<li>
<h3><a href="https://www.hometeamns.sg/tplay/khatib/"><strong>T-Play Khatib&nbsp;</strong></a></h3>
</li>
</ol>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-350 size-full" src="https://sunbird.com.sg/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/dj4mmnppwgqmi0nrloab.webp" alt="" width="843" height="474"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Families in the North, rejoice! T-Play Khatib is a fun-tastic indoor playground located at HomeTeamNS Khatib. This site is especially unique as it is heavily inspired by Southeast Asia’s Peranakan culture, making for an interesting change of scenery for your little one! This facility features unique and exciting play areas, such as a sports pitch, foam ball shooting arena, and a ninja course for your child to unleash their little ninja!</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Location: HomeTeamNS Khatib, 2 Yishun Walk, Singapore 767944</p>
<p>Opening Hours: Monday: CLOSED</p>
<p>Tuesday – Thursday: 1pm – 7pm</p>
<p>Friday, Eve of PH: 1pm – 9pm</p>
<p>Saturday, Sunday, School Holiday &amp; PH: 10am to 9pm</p>
<p>Admission fee: Prices begin from SGD$10 and differ for HomeTeamNS Family members and T-Play Members.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol start="3">
<li>
<h3><a href="https://www.pororoparksg.com/"><strong>Pororo Park&nbsp;</strong></a></h3>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-351 size-full" style="font-size: 16px;" src="https://sunbird.com.sg/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/pororo.jpg" alt="" width="877" height="658"></p></li>
</ol>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Pororo Park is Singapore’s first character-based indoor playground and celebrates the beloved character of Pororo and his friends! Your little one can play pretend at the Petty Supermarket, buying groceries and even cooking a feast at the little kitchen. This indoor playground also includes a Gallop Station, a mini merry-go-round that will delight the hearts of your child! Pororo Park also features a toddler area so that younger children also do not miss out a chance to enjoy the fantastical land that belongs to Pororo and his friends.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Location: 6 Raffles Boulevard, Marina Square #02-29, Singapore 039594</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Opening Hours:</p>
<p>Weekday Play Sessions:</p>
<p>10.30am - 1.30pm</p>
<p>1.45pm - 4.45pm</p>
<p>5.00pm - 8.00pm</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Weekend/PH Play Sessions:</p>
<p>10.30am - 12.30pm</p>
<p>1.00pm - 3.00pm</p>
<p>3.30pm - 5.30pm</p>
<p>6.00pm - 8.00pm</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Admission Fee: SGD$36 each for one child and one accompanying adult</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol start="4">
<li>
<h3><strong><a href="https://www.safra.sg/our-facilities/kidz-amaze">Kids Amaze @ SAFRA</a></strong></h3>
<p>&nbsp;</p></li>
</ol>
<p><img class="aligncenter wp-image-352 size-full" src="https://sunbird.com.sg/wp-content/uploads/2022/09/kidz-amaze-safra-jurong-family.jpg" alt="" width="750" height="500"></p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Kids Amaze @ SAFRA is conveniently located at three different locations - Jurong, Toa Payoh, and Punggol.&nbsp; Kids Amaze @ SAFRA Jurong boasts an 18.5m tall slide and is <em>the </em>tallest Ballocity in Asia! That is no mean feat. Moreover, for the younger children, Kids Amaze @ SAFRA Toa Payoh features the first Infant and Toddler Development Play Zone in Singapore, and highlights the value of edu-tainment, which aims to spotlight opportunities to learn through fun play.</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Address:</p>
<p>SAFRA Jurong</p>
<p>333 Boon Lay Way, Singapore 649848</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>SAFRA Toa Payoh</p>
<p>293 Lor 6 Toa Payoh, Singapore 319387</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>SAFRA Punggol</p>
<p>9 Sentul Cres, Singapore 828654</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Opening Hours: Mon - Fri: 1pm - 7pm</p>
<p>Sat, Sun, PH &amp; Sch Hols: 10am - 7pm</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p>Admission fee: Prices begin from SGD$10, and differ for SAFRA Members</p>
<p>&nbsp;</p>
<ol start="5">
<li>
<h3><a href="https://www.jewelchangiairport.com/en/attractions/canopy-park.html"><strong>Canopy Park &amp; Sky Nets at Jewel Changi Airport</strong></a></h3>
</li>
</ol>
