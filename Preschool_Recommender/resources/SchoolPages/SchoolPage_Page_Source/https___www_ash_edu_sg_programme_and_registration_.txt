            <div class="section-title-bar not-sticky">
            <h1>Registration</h1>
        </div>
    
    
    <div class="uk-section ash-bg-yellow" uk-scrollspy="target: > div; cls: uk-animation-slide-bottom-medium; delay: 150; offset-top: -100">
        <div class="uk-container" style="visibility: hidden;">
            <h5>Eligibility</h5>
<p>Admission for any academic year is according to the age set by the Ministry of Education (MOE). Age is calculated based on 2 January of the year of admission.</p>
<h5>Registration for International Students</h5>
<p>Foreigners (except those with a Dependant Pass/Immigration Exemption Order) are required to apply for a Student Pass. Please contact our Admin Officer at 6562 4932 or login to <a href="http://www.ica.gov.sg" target="_blank" rel="noopener">www.ica.gov.sg</a> for more information.</p>
<h5>Registration</h5>
<p>Please make an admission appointment with our Admin Officer at 6562 4932 prior to your visit. We recommend bringing your child along to facilitate the process.</p>
<p>For a smooth experience, we seek your cooperation to follow the guidelines below.</p>
<h5>Fees Payment</h5>
<ol>
<li>A non-refundable registration fee is required upon registration.</li>
<li>A refundable deposit of 1 month’s school fee (excluding government subsidy) is payable upon registration. Terms &amp; Conditions apply for this refundable deposit.</li>
<li>The first month’s school fees must be paid by NETS / Baby Bonus NETS only. Subsequent monthly school fees must be paid by Baby Bonus (GIRO), Interbank GIRO or NETS.</li>
<li>Singapore Citizen child can use the Baby Bonus to pay for the monthly fees.</li>
<li>Any Singapore Citizen (SC) child enrolled in an ECDA-licensed child care centre will be eligible to receive <a href="https://www.ecda.gov.sg/parents/subsidies-financial-assistance#CCICS" target="_blank" rel="noopener">government subsidies</a>. According to ECDA’s guidelines, children must attend the center at least one day per month to receive the monthly subsidy claim.</li>
</ol>
