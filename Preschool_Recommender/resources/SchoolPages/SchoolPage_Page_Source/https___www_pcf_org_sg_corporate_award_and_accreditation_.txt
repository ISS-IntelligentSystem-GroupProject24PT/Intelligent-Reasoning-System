								<div class="s_text">
																			<h3>Progressive Wage Mark Accreditation</h3>
																												<h4>PCF has obtained the Progressive Wage Mark Accreditation</h4>
																												<p><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">The Progressive Wage (PW) Mark&nbsp;</span><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">symbolises</span><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">&nbsp;excellence for companies that pay fair wages to&nbsp;</span><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">lower-wage workers. This accreditation, administered by the Singapore Business Federation on&nbsp;</span><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">behalf of the Tripartite Partners (MOM, NTUC, SNEF),&nbsp; identifies and supports businesses that&nbsp;</span><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">positively impact the well-being of our workforce.</span></p>
																	</div>
																										<div class="s_showmore">
																					
											<div class="s_col">
																									<div class="s_show">
														<p><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">We are pleased to announce that PCF has obtained the Progressive Wage Mark accreditation.</span>​&nbsp;<span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">With this accreditation, we have fulfilled the new government procurement requirement for firms&nbsp;</span><span data-usefontface="true" data-contrast="none">to be accredited with the PW Mark for the contract period. The PW Mark requirement will be extended to Government quotations from 1 March 2024.</span></p>

							<div class="s_innerwrap">
								<div class="s_text">
																			<h3>WIPO- IPOS IP For Innovation Awards 2023</h3>
																												<h4>The WIPO-IPOS IP For Innovation Awards 2023 celebrates Singapore enterprises who have achieved outstanding growth through the successful use of intellectual property (IP).</h4>
																										</div>
																										<div class="s_showmore">
																					
											<div class="s_col">
																									<div class="s_show">
														<p>Organised by IPOS International and supported by the Intellectual Property Office of Singapore (IPOS) and the World Intellectual Property Organisation (WIPO), the Awards seek to recognise IP champions who have leveraged the effective use of IP to innovate, transform and grow their business.</p>
													</div>
																																					<div class="s_more">
														<p>Since its inception in 2013, 50 organisations have been conferred this pre-eminent accolade. PCF is delighted to announce that we have achieved a significant milestone by becoming One of the Top Six (6) finalists for the prestigious WIPO-IPOS IP for Innovation Awards 2023.</p>
													</div>
																							</div>
																							<div class="s_col v_hidden">
													<p>This distinguished honour underscores our unique approach to measuring success. At PCF, we prioritise the profound impact we have on society over commercial standards. Our commitment to innovation is evident through our leading role in inclusive education research, our proprietary materials in mother tongue, and our renowned ONE PCF Curriculum. Our recognition as finalists in these awards highlights the role of innovation, intangible assets, and intellectual property in shaping the future of our young ones, reaffirming that innovation knows no boundaries, even in the preschool sector.</p>
