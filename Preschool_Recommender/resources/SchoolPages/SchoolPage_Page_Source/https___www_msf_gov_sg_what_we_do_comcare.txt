<div class="accordion" id="accordionExample">
    <div id="Content_C038_Col00" class="sf_colsIn sub-section " data-sf-element="Accordion Content" data-placeholder-label="Accordion Content">

    <div class="accordion-item" id="question_can_we_apply_for_comcare_assistance_if_our_household_income_is_higher_than_the_stated_criteria">
        <h2 class="accordion-header" id="heading_question_can_we_apply_for_comcare_assistance_if_our_household_income_is_higher_than_the_stated_criteria">
            <button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapse_question_can_we_apply_for_comcare_assistance_if_our_household_income_is_higher_than_the_stated_criteria" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse_question_can_we_apply_for_comcare_assistance_if_our_household_income_is_higher_than_the_stated_criteria" aria-label="Question: Can we apply for ComCare assistance if our household income is higher than the stated criteria?">
                Question: Can we apply for ComCare assistance if our household income is higher than the stated criteria?
            </button>
        </h2>
        <div id="collapse_question_can_we_apply_for_comcare_assistance_if_our_household_income_is_higher_than_the_stated_criteria" class="accordion-collapse collapse" aria-labelledby="heading_question_can_we_apply_for_comcare_assistance_if_our_household_income_is_higher_than_the_stated_criteria" data-bs-parent="#accordionExample">
            <div class="accordion-body">
                <strong>Answer:</strong> The income criteria for ComCare schemes are not hard thresholds. Our Social Service Offices (SSOs) assess the applicants' circumstances and needs holistically, provide financial assistance and connect them to community support as needed.
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>



    <div class="accordion-item" id="question_how_long_is_comcare_assistance_given_for">
        <h2 class="accordion-header" id="heading_question_how_long_is_comcare_assistance_given_for">
            <button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapse_question_how_long_is_comcare_assistance_given_for" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse_question_how_long_is_comcare_assistance_given_for" aria-label="Question: How long is ComCare assistance given for?">
                Question: How long is ComCare assistance given for?
            </button>
        </h2>
        <div id="collapse_question_how_long_is_comcare_assistance_given_for" class="accordion-collapse collapse" aria-labelledby="heading_question_how_long_is_comcare_assistance_given_for" data-bs-parent="#accordionExample">
            <div class="accordion-body">
                <strong>Answer:</strong> ComCare Long-Term Assistance is for persons permanently unable to work and have little or no financial means or family support. Short-to-Medium-Term Assistance is typically granted for 3 to 6 months in the first instance, and renewed if the household continues to require support.
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>



    <div class="accordion-item" id="question_does_the_applicant_have_to_be_a_singapore_citizen_to_be_eligible_for_comcare_assistance">
        <h2 class="accordion-header" id="heading_question_does_the_applicant_have_to_be_a_singapore_citizen_to_be_eligible_for_comcare_assistance">
            <button class="accordion-button collapsed" type="button" data-bs-toggle="collapse" data-bs-target="#collapse_question_does_the_applicant_have_to_be_a_singapore_citizen_to_be_eligible_for_comcare_assistance" aria-expanded="true" aria-controls="collapse_question_does_the_applicant_have_to_be_a_singapore_citizen_to_be_eligible_for_comcare_assistance" aria-label="Question: Does the applicant have to be a Singapore Citizen to be eligible for ComCare assistance?">
                Question: Does the applicant have to be a Singapore Citizen to be eligible for ComCare assistance?
            </button>
        </h2>
        <div id="collapse_question_does_the_applicant_have_to_be_a_singapore_citizen_to_be_eligible_for_comcare_assistance" class="accordion-collapse collapse" aria-labelledby="heading_question_does_the_applicant_have_to_be_a_singapore_citizen_to_be_eligible_for_comcare_assistance" data-bs-parent="#accordionExample">
            <div class="accordion-body">
                <strong>Answer:</strong> The applicant must be a Singapore Citizen or Permanent Resident (where at least one immediate family member of the same household is a Singapore Citizen).
            </div>
        </div>
    </div>
