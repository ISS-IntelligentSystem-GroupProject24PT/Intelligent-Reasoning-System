                                <h1>Active Ageing Centre</h1>

                                <div class="group"></div>

                            </div>

                            <div class="document"> <p><span style="color: #ff0000; font-size: 14pt;"><strong>(中文如下)</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Our Active Ageing Centre welcomes various partners from corporate bodies, healthcare establishments, education institutions, food and beverage, hotels, airlines, grassroots, and individuals. </strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;"><strong>Our centre promote active ageing through social integration, meaningful courses, and opportunities where public and seniors can learn new skills and help each other in our community.</strong></span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">我们的乐龄中心欢迎来自企业团体、医疗机构、教育机构、餐饮、酒店、航空公司、和个人的各种合作伙伴。</span></p>
<p><span style="font-size: 14pt;">我们的中心通过社会融合、有意义的课程以及公众和长者可以学习新技能并在社区中互相帮助的机会来促进积极老龄化。</span></p>
