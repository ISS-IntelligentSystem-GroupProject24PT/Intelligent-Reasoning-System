        <p class="bodytexts">
At Sunflower, we welcome professional trained individuals with the desire to inspire young children to develop them successfully. If you think you have what it takes, we want to hear from you, please email us at <a href="mailto:info@sunflowerkiddystar.com">info@sunflowerkiddystar.com</a>

                <h4 style="margin-top: 31px;">Professional Development</h4>
                    <p class="bodytexts" align="left">
                                At Sunflower Kiddy Star Preschool, our educators are involved to plan for their individual professional development to meet their developmental needs. They attended continuous development programme (CDP) to upgrade their skills and keep up to date with the latest development in the early childhood education sector. They conducted research and peer sharing session to further enhanced their teaching and learning practices.

                    </p>
                    
                    <h4 style="margin-top: 32px;">Place and Train Programme</h4>
                    <p class="bodytexts">
                                As part of our social responsibility, we are committed to support the development of early childhood professionals. We welcome untrained potential candidate to join the ECE sector and are happy to sponsor you for the Place and Train programme. You will gain valuable on the job training while pursing your Diploma in Early Childhood Care and Education. Call us at 6789 1033 if you are interested.

                    </p>
                    
                    <h4 style="margin-top: 32px;">Internship Programme</h4>
                    <p class="bodytexts">
                                Sunflower Kiddy Star Preschool participates in internship programme in the early childhood sector to mentor student teachers to develop work-ready skills by providing them with real life meaningful on-the-job experiences to ease the student teachers’ transition into the sector as trained early childhood teachers after they graduate.
