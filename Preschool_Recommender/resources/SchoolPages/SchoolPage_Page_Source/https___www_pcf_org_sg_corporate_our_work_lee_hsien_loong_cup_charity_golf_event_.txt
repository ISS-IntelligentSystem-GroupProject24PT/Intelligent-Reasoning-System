									<p>The Lee Hsien Loong Cup Charity Golf Tournament has been a highlight in the calendar of activities for the Ang Mo Kio GRC since 2004. This annual tournament is organised by the different divisions within the GRC on a rotation basis.</p>
<h3>Funds raised at the tournament are used to:</h3>
<ul>
<li>Help the needy students in PCF Sparkletots Preschools</li>
<li>Support youth activities</li>
<li>Promote activities for senior citizens</li>
<li>Provide community welfare for low-income families</li>
