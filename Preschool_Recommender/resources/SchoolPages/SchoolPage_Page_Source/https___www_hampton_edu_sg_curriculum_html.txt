	<h2>
			Curriculum		</h2>








<h3 class="whitebg_top"><img style="margin: 5px; float: right;" src="/images/stories/curriculum00.jpg" alt=""><span style="font-size: 18pt; color: #e20a16;">Inquiry Curriculum Framework</span></h3>
<div class="whitebg_top">&nbsp;</div>
<div class="whitebg_top">
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">At Hampton Preschool, the children’s contributions, prior knowledge, interests and inquiries, as well as the social and cultural setting, will all shape how a unit of inquiry unfolds. Inquiry is an educational philosophy way of teaching and a unit of inquiry will look different in different schools, in different classrooms and from year to year. During the inquiry process, children will:</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● explore, wonder and question</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● experiment and play with possibilities</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● making and testing theories</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● collect data and report findings</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● solving problems in a variety of ways</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">At school, we also strive to provide an environment where children embrace values, develop social-emotional competencies and flourish to become a caring person who contributes positively in the community.</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum">&nbsp;</p>
<h3><span style="color: #e20a16;"><span style="font-size: 18pt;">Nurturing Early Learners Curriculum Framework</span><span style="font-size: 18pt;">&nbsp;</span></span></h3>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">We carefully curate our children’s learning experiences by adapting Nurturing Early Learners Curriculum Framework designed by the Ministry of Education too. To ensure that the preschoolers grow holistically, six learning areas are integrated seamlessly into the curriculum. They are:</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● Aesthetics and Creative Expression</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● Discovery of the World</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● Language and Literacy</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● Motor Skills Development</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● Numeracy</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">● Social and Emotional Development</span></p>
<p class="about_us_curriculum"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">We encourage parents and family members to participate actively in the children’s learning and create beautiful memories together with us. If you are keen to be a volunteer, please <a href="https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdweyDlHmmdHTCR2QDXDdzKDLio-BnIVxoum4mRhTi5drKQPw/viewform">click here.</a></span></p>
