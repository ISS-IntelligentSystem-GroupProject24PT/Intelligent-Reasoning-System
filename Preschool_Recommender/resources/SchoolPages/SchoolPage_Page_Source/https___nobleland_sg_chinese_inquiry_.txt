<p>每年我们也会利用一个学期开展大型的方案教学, 由孩子做主选择自己喜欢的项目去开展, 在学习中发现问题，想办法解决问题以及寻找答案。<br>
环境是孩子们的第三任老师，我们把自然环境当成第二个课室，他们身边的花草树木，鸟兽虫鱼都是教材，我们鼓励孩子们在周围的环境中去探索，去发现身边无处不在的美。</p>

<h6>Inquiry: Learn from the World</h6>
<div class="row clearfix">
<div class="box three-four ">
Chinese Inquiry Lessons involve classroom projects that use inquiry based approach. It is similar to the English Inquiry Project, but conducted in Chinese. Your child will engage with topics from two major themes that pique their interest and develop an understanding for the world around them.<p></p>
<ol type="1">
<li><a name="discovering"></a>Discovering the World</li>
<ul>
<li>Environmental Issues, Flora and Fauna, World Culture etc. </li>
</ul>
<li><a name="science"></a>Science and Technology</li>
<ul>
<li>Electricity, Vehicles, Light etc.</li>
</ul>
</ol>
<p><a name="understanding"></a><br>
Your child will learn via questioning and researching about the assigned topic, and will be tasked to problem-solve as well. The process is emphasised here and your child will gain experience and knowledge through the Inquiry projects. Our Inquiry projects aim to develop your child’s observational, analytical and problem solving skills.<br>
