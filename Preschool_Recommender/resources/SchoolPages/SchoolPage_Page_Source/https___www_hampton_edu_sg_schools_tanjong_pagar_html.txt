	<h2>
			About Tanjong Pagar		</h2>








<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">Hampton Pre-School, Tanjong Pagar, is a PCF centre managed by EtonHouse, catering to children from 2 months to 6 years of age. The campus has a thoughtfully designed environment that offers children a meaningful and engaging learning experience. Equipped with innovative resources, the pre-school offers a unique curriculum that is based on respect for young children. Hampton Pre-School offers an international early childhood programme that is integrated and holistic and lays the foundation for future success.</span></p>
<p style="text-align: justify;"><span style="font-size: 12pt;">With the mandate from the late Minister Mentor Mr Lee Kuan Yew, Hampton Preschool Tanjong Pagar commenced its operations in January 2014 to provide a quality and affordable preschool services to the community. In 2017, they received their SPARK accreditation. Beyond that, the school achieved national recognition as an Outstanding Centre in Teaching and Learning (Commendation) awarded by the Early Childhood Development Agency (ECDA) in 2021.</span></p>
