<h2><span style="COLOR: #000080"></span>&nbsp;</h2>
<p><span style="COLOR: #000080">3.1 Every Nursery class has two class teachers. 
K1 and K2 classes have 1 class teacher. The class is sometimes divided into two 
groups during language class, art class and also computer class so that more 
attention is given to children.</span></p>
<p><span style="COLOR: #000080"><span><span>3.2 Every class has a specialized 
Art teacher, Computer teacher and the Tamil/Hindi teacher, providing a bilingual 
environment for the children.</span></span></span></p>
<h2><span style="COLOR: #000080"><br>4. Admission</span></h2>
<p><span style="COLOR: #000080">Admission for any academic year is governed by 
the age stipulated by the Ministry of Education. Age is calculated based in 1 
January of the year of admission.</span></p>
<ul>
  <li><span style="COLOR: #000080">Nursery : Year that the child turns 4</span> 
  </li><li><span style="COLOR: #000080">Kindergarten : Year that the child turns 
  5</span> 
  </li><li><span style="COLOR: #000080">Kindergarten : Year that the child turns 
  6</span> </li></ul>
<p><span style="COLOR: #000080"></span>&nbsp;</p>
