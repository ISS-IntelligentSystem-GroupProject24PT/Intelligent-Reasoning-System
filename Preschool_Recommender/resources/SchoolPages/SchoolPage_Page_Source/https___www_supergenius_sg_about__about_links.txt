          <div class="sqs-layout sqs-grid-12 columns-12" data-type="page" data-updated-on="1583637359310" id="page-5c18b90a4ae2373aab169f4e"><div class="row sqs-row"><div class="col sqs-col-4 span-4"><div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-5c18b90a4ae2373aab169f4f"><div class="sqs-block-content">

<div class="sqs-html-content">
  <h2 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">About SuperGenius</h2>
</div>






















</div></div></div><div class="col sqs-col-8 span-8"><div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-yui_3_17_2_1_1545196760436_18129"><div class="sqs-block-content">

<div class="sqs-html-content">
  <h3 style="white-space:pre-wrap;">A Preschool inspired by SG</h3><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">SuperGenius is inspired by Singapore (SG). Founded on the belief that creativity and innovation are essential ingredients for success and making a positive impact on society, SuperGenius Preschool aims to inspire the SuperGenius in children aged 2 months to 6 years old by expanding their horizons and enhancing their perceptions.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">In 2012, <a href="http://www.stalford.edu.sg" target="_blank">Stalford Learning Centre</a> had launched a holiday enrichment programme for preschoolers titled “The SuperGenius” that encompasses phonics, mathematics, and science experimentation. The success of the holiday enrichment programme kick-started a conversation amongst the <a href="#team">Maestros</a>, and intrigued us to further explore the definition of a “SuperGenius”, and create a preschool that we would want to send our children to learn how to be a SuperGenius.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Ruby, founder of SuperGenius Preschool and co-founder of Stalford Education Group, aspires to develop a preschool education&nbsp;curriculum&nbsp;that would distinctly position Singapore as a leader in preschool education on the world stage. </p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">Evolving from an academically-driven holiday enrichment programme, the Maestros harnessed the best of early childhood education approaches and methodologies from around the world, and developed a SuperGenius Programme for preschoolers that would nurture learning attributes and build character attributes; attributes that define the SuperGenius. <a href="/programme#supergenius-profile">The four learning attributes of Joyful, Agile, Inquisitive, and Reflective, and the four character attributes of Honest, Resilient, Caring, and Grateful forms the SuperGenius Profile.</a> A SuperGenius is thus defined by these eight profile attributes.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">The Maestros firmly believe that Every Child is a SuperGenius, and a trans-domain Reggio-inspired programme that is contextualised for Singapore is the most suitable approach to Inspire the SuperGenius. <a href="/programme#programme-areas">The SuperGenius Programme comprises six trans-domain Programme Areas that includes SuperGenius Moves, Languages &amp; Theatre, ArtScience, Well-being, Environmental Sustainability and Emergent Experiences.</a> The programme aims to nurture the SuperGenius attributes and inspire children to realise their potential and be responsible global citizens.</p><p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">SuperGenius Preschool is an amalgamation of the Maestros’ vision and extensive research on the inquiry-based approach and facilitation methods adapted to suit Singapore’s context. We seek to provide the right <a href="/programme#learning-environment">Learning Environment</a> and education resources to develop every child to be the best version of themselves.
